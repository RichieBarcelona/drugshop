About Miran Drug Shop

Discover our journey from a small local pharmacy to becoming a trusted healthcare partner in our community. Learn about our mission, values, and the dedicated team behind our success.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Richie Barcelona, Miran Drug Shop began with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide accessible healthcare solutions to our community.

What started as a small local pharmacy has grown into a comprehensive healthcare destination, serving thousands of families with quality pharmaceutical products and professional healthcare services.

Our founder's vision was clear from the beginning - to create a place where people could not only purchase medications but also receive expert advice and compassionate care. This vision continues to drive everything we do today.
