Frequently Asked Questions

Do you require prescriptions for all medications?

No, we carry both prescription and over-the-counter medications. Prescription medications require a valid prescription from a licensed healthcare provider.

Can I order medications online?

Currently, we offer in-store and phone ordering. Online ordering will be available soon for your convenience.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer local delivery services for prescription medications. Please contact us for delivery options and fees.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and most major insurance plans. We also offer flexible payment options for larger orders.
