About MDS

Our Story

MDS was founded in 2020 by Richie Barcelona. Our mission is to provide accessible healthcare solutions to our community through a comprehensive selection of pharmaceutical products.

When did your area first get a drug shop?

Richie Barcelona conducted research and discovered that Miran Drug Shop was founded in 2020, becoming the first drug shop in the area. This marked a significant milestone in providing accessible healthcare to the local community.
